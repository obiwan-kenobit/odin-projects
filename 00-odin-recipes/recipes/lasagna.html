Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a traditional Italian dish made of layers of pasta, meat or vegetable sauce, and cheese, baked until golden and bubbling.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Ground beef or pork (or a mix)
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Italian herbs (basil, oregano)
  • Ricotta or béchamel sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Prepare the sauce: sauté chopped onion and garlic in olive oil, add ground meat and brown it. Add tomato sauce, salt, pepper, and herbs. Simmer 20-30 minutes.
  2. Prepare béchamel (optional if using ricotta): melt butter, stir in flour, then gradually add milk until smooth. Season with salt and nutmeg.
  3. Cook lasagna sheets: boil in salted water if not pre-cooked, then drain and lay on a towel.
  4. Assemble: layer sauce, pasta, béchamel or ricotta, mozzarella, and parmesan in a baking dish. Repeat layers, finishing with cheese on top.
  5. Bake: cover with foil and bake at 180-200°C for 25-30 minutes. Remove foil and bake 10-15 more minutes until golden.
  6. Rest and serve: let sit 10 minutes before cutting and serving.
