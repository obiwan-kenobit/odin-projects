Pizza

Description

Pizza is an Italian dish made with a flat round dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and various ingredients, baked in a hot oven until crisp and melted.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (flour, water, yeast, salt, olive oil)
  • Tomato sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  • Oregano or basil
  • Toppings of choice (e.g. ham, mushrooms, vegetables)

Steps

  1. Prepare the dough: mix flour, yeast, salt, water, and olive oil. Knead until smooth and let rise for 1–2 hours.
  2. Preheat the oven to 250°C (480°F).
  3. Roll out the dough on a floured surface and place it on a baking tray or stone.
  4. Spread tomato sauce evenly, then add mozzarella and desired toppings.
  5. Bake for 8–12 minutes until crust is golden and cheese melted.
  6. Drizzle with olive oil, add fresh basil if desired, and serve hot.
