Arrosto

Description

Roast is a classic meat dish cooked slowly in the oven, often seasoned with herbs, garlic, and vegetables to create a tender and flavorful result.

Ingredients

  • Beef or pork roast (about 1–1.5 kg)
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Garlic cloves
  • Rosemary and thyme
  • Onion
  • Carrots
  • Celery
  • White wine or broth

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Rub the meat with salt, pepper, and olive oil. Add garlic and herbs.
  3. Place in a roasting pan with chopped vegetables.
  4. Pour in a bit of wine or broth to keep it moist.
  5. Roast for about 1.5–2 hours, basting occasionally.
  6. Let rest 10 minutes before slicing and serving with the vegetables and sauce.
