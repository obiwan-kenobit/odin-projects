Sushi

Description

Sushi is a Japanese dish made with vinegared rice combined with raw or cooked seafood, vegetables, or egg, often wrapped in seaweed or served as small rolls or nigiri.

Ingredients

  • Sushi rice
  • Rice vinegar
  • Sugar
  • Salt
  • Nori seaweed sheets
  • Fresh fish (salmon, tuna, etc.)
  • Vegetables (cucumber, avocado, carrot)
  • Soy sauce
  • Wasabi
  • Pickled ginger

Steps

  1. Rinse and cook the rice. Let it cool slightly.
  2. Mix rice vinegar, sugar, and salt, then fold into the rice.
  3. Place a nori sheet on a bamboo mat, spread rice evenly, and leave a small edge.
  4. Add fillings such as fish and vegetables.
  5. Roll tightly using the mat, then cut into slices with a wet knife.
  6. Serve with soy sauce, wasabi, and pickled ginger.
